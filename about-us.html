About Us

Anand Gurukul Residential Program (AGRP) is designed to provide students with a disciplined and supportive ecosystem for NEET and JEE preparation. The campus combines hostel comfort, mentorship, daily monitoring, and focused self-study so students can build strong academic habits and confidence.

Our objective is to give every student a structured routine, quality environment, and continuous guidance under one roof so they can work toward their target examination with clarity and consistency.
