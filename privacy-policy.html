Privacy Policy

Anand Gurukul Residential Program (AGRP)

Anand Gurukul Residential Program (AGRP) respects the privacy of its students, parents, and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information related to students enrolled in the Anand Gurukul Residential Program. By enrolling in or interacting with AGRP, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. About AGRP

Anand Gurukul Residential Program (AGRP) is a structured residential program designed for students preparing for NEET and JEE entrance examinations. The program provides a disciplined, focused, and supportive environment that enables students to prepare effectively for competitive exams and achieve admission into their desired colleges.

AGRP offers a comprehensive residential experience where students live in a structured academic environment similar to a coaching institute. Students benefit from a study-friendly hostel atmosphere, guided self-study sessions, routine monitoring, mentorship support, nutritious food, and a disciplined daily routine that promotes both academic success and personal growth.

2. Information We Collect

To operate the residential program effectively, AGRP may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Student name, age, date of birth
  • Parent or guardian name and contact details
  • Residential address
  • Phone number and email address
  • Identification documents if required during admission

Academic Information

  • School details
  • Academic records relevant to NEET/JEE preparation
  • Performance and assessment reports within the program

Health and Safety Information

  • Basic medical information necessary for hostel safety
  • Emergency contact details
  • Dietary requirements or allergies (if provided)

Hostel and Program Information

  • Room allocation records
  • Attendance and routine monitoring details
  • Participation in academic and mentoring activities

3. How We Use the Information

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Managing student admissions and registrations
  • Maintaining hostel accommodation records
  • Ensuring student safety and well-being
  • Providing academic monitoring and mentorship
  • Communicating with parents or guardians
  • Managing food services and hostel facilities
  • Improving the quality of the residential program

AGRP uses student information strictly for educational and administrative purposes related to the residential program.

4. Data Protection and Security

AGRP takes reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. Administrative and technical safeguards are implemented to maintain the confidentiality and security of student data.

Only authorized staff members involved in administration, mentoring, and student welfare are allowed to access necessary information.

5. Information Sharing

AGRP does not sell or rent personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only in limited situations such as:

  • Communication with parents or legal guardians
  • Medical assistance during emergencies
  • Compliance with legal or regulatory requirements
  • Academic coordination related to the student's preparation

All such sharing is done responsibly and only when necessary.

6. Student Safety and Monitoring

Since AGRP is a residential program designed to support disciplined preparation for NEET and JEE examinations, routine monitoring and supervision may be conducted to maintain a safe and structured environment. This includes supervision of study routines, hostel discipline, and mentoring activities.

These practices are implemented solely to support students' academic progress and personal development.

7. Food and Hostel Facilities

AGRP provides students with safe hostel accommodation and clean, nutritious food. Information regarding dietary preferences or restrictions may be collected only to ensure proper meal planning and student well-being.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

AGRP may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect operational improvements or regulatory requirements. Any changes will be communicated through official channels.
