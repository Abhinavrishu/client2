About ANAND GURUKUL

(All Under One Roof Campus)

AGRP is a residential program designed for students preparing for NEET / JEE entrance examinations. Its aim is to provide students with a disciplined, focused, and supportive environment that helps them prepare effectively and get selected in their dream colleges. This program is designed to give students a comprehensive residential experience, where they can stay in a structured system similar to a coaching institute and prepare for their studies through Anand Gurukul’s Residential Program. AGRP students are provided with safe and affordable hostel facilities in a study-friendly environment that supports their academic preparation as well as personal growth. Students also receive daily routine monitoring and guided self-study sessions to strengthen their preparation. Anand Gurukul’s Residential Program provides students with clean and nutritious food, a disciplined lifestyle, and 24×7 mentorship support. The aim is to create a nurturing environment where students can focus on their academic goals while also developing life skills, self-discipline, and personal growth.
