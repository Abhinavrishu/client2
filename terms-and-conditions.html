Terms & Conditions - Anand Gurukul

  • Students at Anand Gurukul may choose to avail only the hostel facility if they wish. The AGRP program is optional.
  • AGRP is a Residential Guidance Program, not a coaching institute.
  • For hostel admission, students must pay 1 month hostel rent + security deposit + maintenance charges.
  • Maintenance charges are payable only once per year.
  • The security deposit is non-refundable under any circumstances. It will only be adjusted in April-March cycle.
  • Hostel fees and AGRP program fees can be paid separately or together.
  • Late fees may be charged if payments are not made on time.
  • If a student violates discipline rules, the management has the authority to cancel the admission.
  • Any form of misconduct or indiscipline will not be tolerated at Anand Gurukul.
  • If a student decides to leave the hostel, a one-month prior notice must be given.
  • Anand Gurukul management reserves the right to modify rules and regulations whenever necessary.
